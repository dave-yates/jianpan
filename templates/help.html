Type pinyin and then select the character you want from the options one at a time.

Enter selects an option.

Tab, shift tab and the left right arrows navigate the options.

Use backspace to undo character selections.

Lower case only

1,2,3,4, 5 can be used for the tones. 5 is neutral tone.

'w', 'wo' and 'wo3' can all be used to search for ‘我‘ (I or me).

eg - This is the character I'm looking for.

zhe4 ge4 shi4 wo3 zhao3 de5 han4 zi4

這個是我找的漢字
